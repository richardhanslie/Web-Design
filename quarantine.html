Quarantine Mode

Prologue


An Unknown Lifeform suddenly emerged, contaminate everything
in its path.
What is "it"? Is this the "End"?

Fight your way as an experienced soldier in this co-op
PvE as you strive to live and
hopefully, save the world


Attack
Attack Operation on the Unknown's bases will give you loots for Researching and upgrading your weapon technology.

Attacking a higher level enemy base gives you more loots as the difficulty is harder.
Choose between sneak attack or just ram thorugh the front door. Sneak attacking is best if you are not sure enough in your firepower. Your fight, your way.
Defend
Successfull defend from the unknown's Invasion will give you research point and information about the enemy.
This information will be used to identify enemy's characteristic, such as skills, health, weakness, etc.
Generally, you want to gather and process information first before commencing attack operation to increase success chance.
Research
As you making contact with the unknown, you gain research point. Upgrade your intel using Research Point.
